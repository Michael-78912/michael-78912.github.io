About Futurevoid

Kelowna-based metalcore.

Hey there!

Who are we?

We're a metalcore band from Kelowna BC! We've been making music since 2023, and put out our first single, HELLHEAD in July 2024.

Since then, we've been working on refining our songs, production, and live performances and are ready to start making some waves in the scene.

In March, 2025, we released our debut E.P. In Vitro which can be found on all streaming platforms!

Recently, we've stepped it up a notch with the heavy single BIG JAMO and an upcoming, emotional track CYANIDE. We've got a whole lot more in the works and aren't slowing down anytime soon.
