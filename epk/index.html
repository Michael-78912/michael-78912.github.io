About FUTUREVOID

FUTUREVOID is a four-piece heavy/metalcore band from Kelowna, BC. Known for brutal live energy and raw, emotionally charged music, the band is quickly building a reputation across the BC Metal scene... Read more

Want the full story? Formed in late 2023, FUTUREVOID blends metalcore, post-hardcore, and modern heavy music into a distinct, high-impact sound. Entirely DIY, the band writes, records, produces, and mixes all their own music. They released their debut single HELLHEAD in May 2024, played their first live show in August 2024, and followed with several singles leading to their debut EP in February 2025. Since then, FUTUREVOID has kept up a steady stream of releases — maintaining both quality and momentum well into 2026.

Members

  • Logan – Vocals: Combines melodic hooks with brutal, fast, and heavy delivery. Balances intense breakdowns with anthemic choruses to drive the energy of every show.
  • Charlie – Drums: Brings power, groove, and precision. A versatile player who knows when to push the band and when to anchor the performance.
  • Sam – Lead Guitar: Offers a unique perspective drawn from alternative music outside the metal scene. Provides distinct songwriting and arrangements that set FUTUREVOID apart.
  • Michael – Rhythm Guitar & Production: Anchors the band with solid riffs while mixing and mastering the tracks, giving FUTUREVOID its polished yet heavy sound.

Business Strategy: FUTUREVOID approaches each release and show with a professional and strategic mindset. The band prioritizes partnerships with key promoters, festivals, and press outlets, leveraging social campaigns, targeted fan engagement, and press relationships. Each release is timed for maximum impact, and live shows are carefully chosen to deliver value for both fans and promoters. Contact us for inquiries — we respond fast and work collaboratively to make every show a success.
